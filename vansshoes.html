Where It All Began

Brothers Paul Van Dorean and Jim Van Dorean along with business partners Gordon Lee and Serge Delia opend the first Vans store in Anaheim, California on March 16th, 1966. On the very first day the company sold a total of 12 shoes. As the business partners began developing new styles the brand continued to become more popular. By the end of the 70's, Vans had 70 stores in California making sells all across the nation and internationally.

More then Just a Shoe Company

Vans is not only known for their shoes, but a wide varity of other goods. Vans offers shoes, clothing, and accessories. Vans has been a supporter of action sports including skateboarding, surf, BMX, and snowboarding.
